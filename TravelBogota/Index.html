Why visit Bogota ?

The heart of Colombia

Bogota is a place that displays diversity. When you are in the downtown the people are playing Latin American music, dancing, drawing portraits, and the landscape is colorful because is painting with Murals all over the place by street artists. So, it is a space immersed in art that even it was appointed by UNESCO in 2012 as the music capital. As well you can find sellers everywhere with their stalls selling typical food like empanadas with aji. Also, Bogota offers all kind of plans from nature tu nocturnal life.
