Location

Bogota

The place is in the Colombia center, is a mountainous terrain but at the same time there are several buildings and houses. Normally, the main streets have potholes and is surrounded by many industries and important centers. Bogota has a large number of wetlands.Also, the city has a cold mountain climate determined by the altitude, whose temperatures range between -3 and 18 °C, with an average annual temperature of 13 °C. The sunniest month is August.
