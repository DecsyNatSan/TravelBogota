Industry

Currency: Colombian peso.
The economy of this country is focused on agriculture. However, the capital has a different dynamic and the economy is based on commerce, financial institutions, services business, and manufacturing industries. These industries in majority are small business under informality. The reason to operate under informality is because of the high unemployment index. Then, it is quite common to find people selling products in public transportation or in the streets.
